Hotel Turmeric

Our History

Hotel Turmeric was born out of a passion for hospitality and a deep love for Erode

Established by Dr. Gauri Shankar, the visionary behind the renowned Atrium in Erode, our hotel was designed to bring luxury and comfort to the heart of this vibrant city. Since opening our doors, we have been committed to providing an exceptional guest experience, blending traditional Indian warmth with modern amenities. Over the years, Hotel Turmeric has grown to become Erode’s premier destination for both business and leisure travelers, always staying true to our mission of offering unparalleled service and a home away from home.

Check-In/Out Policy

Check-in and check-out are at 12 Noon. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

Non-Guaranteed Reservations

All non-guaranteed bookings are held tentatively until 48 hours prior to arrival. If a deposit or company guarantee letter is not received, the hotel reserves the right to release the reservation.

Cancellation Policy

Cancellations made less than 48 hours before arrival will incur a charge of one night’s room rate plus applicable taxes. For group bookings of more than 5 rooms, cancellations must be made at least 7 days in advance.

Payment Policy

100% advance payment is required before check-in, or payment must be made directly at the time of check-in/check-out.

Confirmation

Please send acceptance of the rate letter along with the company seal and signature to confirm your booking.
